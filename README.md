## 🌟 The Vision
**Student Guardian** is more than just a tracker; it’s a behavioral analytics powerhouse. Designed for the modern student, it bridges the gap between high-intensity academic work and essential self-care. By analyzing daily habits—ranging from deep study sessions to spiritual mindfulness and physical activity—it provides a data-driven "Burnout Score" to keep you in the optimal productivity zone.

## ✨ Core Features

### 🔐 Secure Authentication
A multi-user system featuring persistent registration and session-based login. Your data stays private, secure, and accessible only to you.

### 📊 Behavioral Logging
Track your day across **6 critical categories**:
- 📖 **Academic Study:** Quantify your deep work.
- 📱 **Screen Time:** Monitor passive entertainment and social media impact.
- 🚶 **Exercise/Walking:** Value your physical movement.
- 🎨 **Hobbies/ECA:** Track activities that recharge your mind.
- 🕌 **Spiritual Well-being:** Integrated 5-times Daily Salat (Prayer) tracker.
- 😴 **Sleep & Waste:** Balance recovery against unproductive hours.

### 📅 Interactive History Explorer (JList Style)
A premium calendar-based interface. Browse through past entries, select a date, and watch the system reconstruct your "Daily Story" with a full point-by-point breakdown.

### 📈 Long-term Analytics & Smart Advice
Visualize your journey with average performance metrics, peak study hours, and an **AI-powered Advice Engine** that warns you of burnout risks before they happen.

## 🧠 The "Guardian" Algorithm
The system calculates your **Net Performance Score** using a sophisticated weighting system designed to encourage positive habits:

| Activity | Impact | Points |
| :--- | :---: | :--- |
| **Walking / Exercise** | 🟢 | **+12 pts / hr** |
| **Academic Study** | 🟢 | **+10 pts / hr** |
| **Hobbies / ECA** | 🟢 | **+8 pts / hr** |
| **Salat (Prayer)** | 🟢 | **+5 pts / prayer** |
| **Recovery (Sleep)** | 🟢 | **+5 pts / hr** |
| **Travel / Wasted Time** | 🔴 | **-5 pts / hr** |
| **Screen Time** | 🔴 | **-15 pts / hr** |
